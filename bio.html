Alex Berns is a Research Intern at SB Intuitions, SoftBank in Tokyo, Japan, and a Computer Science student at the Graduate School of Information Science, Tohoku University, in Sendai, Japan. He received a B.Eng. in Electrical Engineering from Hochschule Trier. He received the FunkenSprüher Award for his innovative bachelor thesis and a full scholarship from the Ito Foundation to study in Japan. Alongside his studies, he won Germany's national Chinese-language competition and represented Germany in China. He has published two papers at international conferences on Federated Learning and AI Safety.
